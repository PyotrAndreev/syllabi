Production-Grade Python

Do you know how to turn Python code into a production system?
In 101 Production-Grade Python, you’ll learn how to design, build, test, package, deploy, and debug real Python services. We’ll cover at least APIs, databases, brokers, Docker, CI/CD and system design, so you can see the whole architecture, explain your decisions, and rebuild your own project.
This course is for juniors and middles who want to move from “it works on my machine” to “I can ship, scale, and support it.”

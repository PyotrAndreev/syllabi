# CPython Advanced

This course is an invitation into the CPython community – an art-journey.  
We’ll start from advanced Python and step by step move toward its core – CPython.  
You’ll see how engineering decisions are made, understand why and how different parts of the language evolve, and where it is heading next.  


The ideal way to complete the course is by giving your own talk at a conference/meetup or making a contribution to the core of the language.  
There will be a lot of Computer Science foundations, discussions, and hands-on practice.  
We will build CPython from source, profile it, run experiments, and look at the latest changes in the interpreter.  
By joining the course, you’ll see how you can become part of one of the largest open-source projects in the world.  
To deepen your immersion in Python culture, the course is complemented with music and visual art about CPython, helping you understand and remember the material better.  
The art is created along the way and reflects the course topics and the tricky points for students.  
A music album is planned after the course, and students will influence its content.
